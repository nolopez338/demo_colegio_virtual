Review notes. The workbooks use consistent Sources / Subjects / Curriculum / Progression sheets, but column labels vary by area; some term rows include merged or blank trailing cells; Statistics includes grades 0–5 as integrated/non-standalone while term plans begin at grade 6; Theology/Religion appears in Art-Music sources but not as a full standalone workbook; Art-Music also contains Motor Development and Theology rows, so this dashboard treats workbook names as program areas and subject names as subareas.
